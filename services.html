Good to know

Clear expectations before work begins.

How is the final price decided?

It depends on the number of pages, amount of content, requested features, image preparation and overall level of detail.

Is hosting really included?

The first year of basic static hosting and domain registration can be included in the project price. Renewal costs are discussed before the first renewal date.

Can I request changes before launch?

Yes. You will review the website before it goes live. The agreed number of revision rounds should be written into your quote or project agreement.

Can you update the website later?

Yes. Future revisions and additions can be quoted according to the work required.
