Why have a website?

Your website is your business’s online home.

Social media is a great way to share updates, show your work and connect with customers. Your website gives people one clear place to learn about your business when they are ready to take the next step.

It helps potential customers find important information quickly, builds trust and gives you an online presence that is not dependent on a social media platform.

“I already have Facebook and Instagram.”

Social media and a website work best together. They serve different purposes.

Social media

  • Share regular updates
  • Show recent work
  • Connect with followers
  • Build a community

Your website

  • Builds professional trust
  • Explains your services clearly
  • Helps people find you on Google
  • Keeps information in one place

Good to know

Clear expectations before work begins.

How is the final price decided?

It depends on the number of pages, amount of content, requested features, image preparation and overall level of detail.

Is hosting really included?

The first year of basic static hosting and domain registration can be included in the project price. Renewal costs are discussed before the first renewal date.

Can I request changes before launch?

Yes. You will review the website before it goes live. The agreed number of revision rounds should be written into your quote or project agreement.

Can you update the website later?

Yes. Future revisions and additions can be quoted according to the work required.
